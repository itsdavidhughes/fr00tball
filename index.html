What is fr00tball?

We're offering you a once in a lifetime opportunity to play football in a slightly competitive, non-judgemental environment.

You don't have to be a pro, in fact if you've never played at all, we'd love you to come along for a kickabout and enjoy the fun.

Spectators and cheerleaders are more than welcome, in fact they would be greatly appreciated.


Where is it?

We'll be playing at the Gresham Sports Centre. One of the best sporting facilities in Nottingham. There are even changing rooms at the venue. No expense spared.


Best way to get there?

From the center of town, it's best to get the Tram or a taxi. Here's some walking directions from the nearest tram stop (Wilford Village Tram Stop).


Sounds good. Do I need a full team?

No, not at all. Individuals are more than welcome. Register as a solo and we can pick more teams on the day. However teams are welcome if you can round up a minimum of 5 folk.
